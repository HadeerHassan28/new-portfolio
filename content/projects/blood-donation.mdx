# Blood Donation App - ITI Graduation Project

A comprehensive blood donation platform designed to connect blood donors with organizations and spread awareness about blood donation. This ITI graduation project features dual registration systems, location-based services, and integrated communication tools.

## Overview

The Blood Donation App serves as a bridge between blood donors and healthcare organizations, facilitating efficient blood donation processes while educating users about blood types, donation procedures, and safety guidelines. The platform supports both individual users and organizations with specialized features for each.
